Best Messages

发表于 2025-10-21 分类于 Idea collection Waline： 阅读次数：

英文草稿，可能准备在外教课上讲，语法错误等勿计较，还会修改。

Birthday is a mental challange for me every year. I seldom celebrate my birthday, and I seldom told the date to ohty guys as well. Why? Maybe because I’m a shy person. If so many people celebrate my birthday, I will feel nervous, and if I shout to everyone that ‘Today is my birthday!’,but nobody notice me , then I will feel even more akward. However, When I was in my final year of senior high school, there was a tradition in our class, that when you would have a birthday, you should put a box on a desk at the front of the classroom and tell students that your birthday will come. Then your classmates will put their cards in your box as gifts. Obviously, That was really a challange for me. But I also knew that this chance is really precious becouse after one month, we will attend the gaokao. So I put the box on the desk, then in a trembling voice, I shouted that ‘Hello everyone, Tomorrow is my birthday, welcome all of you writing me birthday cards.’ After saying this, I rushed back to my seat. Okay, then the only thing I can do is waiting. I picked up my pen, but I couldn’t write anything. A few minutes later, another student entered my sight. She held a large jar full of candies in her left hand, and another box in her right hand. ‘What will she do?’i wondered. Then she put the jar and the box on the desk, then turned her face to the class, saying,”Tomorrow is my birthday as well, and anyone who write me a birthday card will get a candy.” What? Our birthday was at the same day! Insatantly every student in class looked at her, and the whole class erupted in excitement.
Later, several students put their cards in her box, and then, there were more and more cards in her box. But I didn’t see anyone write a card for me. In the afternoon, she brought another jar,putting it on the desk. This time, she said,”There is another jar of candy! Welcome for sending me birthday cards! And…” She stopped, looking at me: “tomorrow is also the birthday of Suo Zhongkai, welcome for writing cards for him!” After hearing my name, I was shocked. I had believed that there would nobody notice me, but she did. I was really grateful, and at the same time, I thought ‘this time maybe my classmates will write cards for me.’ After class in the evening, when other students left the classroom, I walked to the desk, then opened my box, and …………only five cards in it.
Okay, that was my fault, that was absolutely my fault! Nobody liked me, so why I still did this stupid thing? I was gonna cry, but I held back my tears, locked the door, and left the classroom.

The next day, my birthday finally came, but I wasn’t happy. All day long, I didn’t look at that box at all. It left me miserable. In the evening, a friend of the other class came to me, smiling, giving me a small pack, and said ‘it’s a surprise.’ I was grateul, even though only a few people were willing to write me cards. ‘That’s enough. ‘I told myself. After I returned to my dorm. I sat on my bed, looking at that box and the small pack, I smiled. Then I opened the box——

Dozens of cards lay inside the box. Dozens of cards lay inside the box. I read them with my trembling hands,one by one. one classmate write a long and truthful paragraph,and another classmate drew a cute picture for me, and……. all of them were really kind. Then I opened the small pack. To my surprise, there were ten another cards inside. Later I knew that my friend told his classmates about my birthday, and these cards were written by them! Lying on the bed,I laughed. These were the best messages I’ve ever received.
